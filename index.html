My Goal: Only do work that I enjoy.

I love to automate. So if i am given a task that i find is tiresome or requires my attention but not my creative energy, I will automate that task.
My father Told me to never do the same job twice. This is echoed in basic computer sicence, "DRY - Dont Repeat Yourself"
What I have done so far is taken time to automate any portion of any task that I am given, because Automation gives you a hyper specialized tool to
Solve a problem or make an informed decision, with significantly less time input and uncertainty than otherwise allowable.

Collecting tools of creation

Designers and Developers solve different types of problems in different ways. But a problem in a design field need know no such boundary, so why only specialize in the type of problem you understand how to solve? I desire to be able to solve any problem I come across during the development of my vision. So, by focusing on only learning the skills and patterns inherant in as many different fields of creation and problem solving as possible, and let memorization and calculation be automatically preformed, I find myself able to recognize macropatterns in human creation. I use that knowledge to synthesize cross-disciplinary solutions to problems. Solutions that, on average, closer to the absolute simplest possible solution given current technology.
